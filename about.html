ABOUT US

Welcome to Moriden – the ultimate hub for Fortnite players and fans! We are a passionate team dedicated to building a vibrant community where Fortnite gamers from all around the world can connect, compete, and thrive.

Our app offers everything a Fortnite player needs: the latest news, game updates, pro tips, and event coverage—all in one place. Whether you're looking to squad up, share your victories, or stay ahead of the meta, our platform is designed to keep you in the game and in the loop.

But we don’t stop there. At Moriden, we also bring you a curated collection of high-quality Fortnite merchandise and gaming gear. From stylish apparel to exclusive collectibles, we’ve got everything you need to showcase your love for the game—both in and out of the Battle Bus.
